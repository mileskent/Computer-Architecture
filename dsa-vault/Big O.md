### Common Functions
Ordered worst to best.
- $O(n^n)$
	- Shit
- $O(n!)$
	- Factorial
- $O(2^n)$
	- Exponential
- $O(n^2)$
	- Quadratic
- $O(n\ log\ n)$
- $O(n)$ 
	- Linear
- $O(log\ n)$
	- Logarthmic
- $O(1)$
	- Constant
